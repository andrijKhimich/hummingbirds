What is Hummingbird Warriors

The Hummingbird Warriors NFT collection was created on the Ethereum blockchain , using an uncommon erc721a standard ; this allows us to reduce commission for our users. The difference becomes especially significant when you mint two or more NFTs per transaction. Rest assured, our team wrote the contract with the highest possible quality and optimization.

Our goal is to make the process easier for you, while also minimising the negative impact NFT-industry has on the environment.

Roadmap

Hummingbird Warriors Video Game

Hummingbird Warriors is honored to have secured Partnership with One Tree Planted . Through this partnership we have the ability to plant over 100,000 trees around the globe with each collection we create. This is an effective way to plant a large amount of trees at once and make a real difference for the future. The community can help vote where we plant. Show more

Hummingbird Warriors Video Game

Each Hummingbird Warrior NFT will be an avatar playing in the game. Our birds will combat deforestation, have treasure hunts, and much more.

Partnership with car companies

A partnership with car companies like Ferrari, Lamborghini and BMW to make a Hummingbird Warrior special edition car that is a symbol of helping the planet.

Each car sold would plant trees and owners would select their Hummingbird Warrior to be placed on the car. We can host racing events on both Metaverse properties.

Hummingbird Coin

Our goal is to eventually create our own Coin to be used as currency in our Metaverse properties and the video game. Active Hummingbird Warriors NFT owners will share a percentage of profits to earn real income.

Frequently asked questions

NFT is a non-fungible token, which is a proof of ownership recorded on a blockchain. Non-fungible means that each NFT is unique and, unlike cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin or Dogecoin, cannot be substituted or equally exchanged for each other. Anything can be an NFT, but usually it means some kind of digital artwork.
